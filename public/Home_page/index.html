FAQ

Common questions

Here are the most common questions that we get.
What types of plant diseases can be detected? The website's frontend is designed to detect a wide range of plant diseases, including fungal infections, bacterial infections, viral infections, nutrient deficiencies, and other common plant ailments.
How does the plant disease detection process work? he plant disease detection process involves analyzing uploaded plant images using machine learning algorithms. These algorithms compare the characteristics of the uploaded image with a database of known plant diseases to identify potential diseases and provide predictions.
Can I upload multiple images for disease detection? Currently, the website allows users to upload one image at a time for disease detection. If you have multiple images to analyze, you can repeat the process for each image separately.
How accurate are the disease predictions? The accuracy of disease predictions can vary depending on various factors, such as the quality of the uploaded image and the complexity of the disease. The website strives to provide accurate predictions but it is important to note that it is not a substitute for professional diagnosis and advice from a plant expert.
Is my uploaded image stored or used for any other purposes? The website does not store or use uploaded images for any purposes other than disease detection. Your privacy and data security are important to us.
